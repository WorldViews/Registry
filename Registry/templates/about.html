Mission

To support the Human family living on this one beautiful planet in sharing the many views of our world.  

 

This involves providing easy access to technologies that allows us to easily see other parts of the world, and to share our own, in many exciting ways, such as live or recorded images, video, and virtual models. More importantly, it involves using those technologies to help us connect, and to share our stories, concerns and dreams. {% endblock %}
